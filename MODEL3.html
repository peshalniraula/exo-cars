TESLA MODEL 3

Safety is the most vital feature of the overall Model 3 structure. The metal design is a mixture of aluminum and steel, for maximum strength in each and every part of the Model 3. In a roof-breakage test, Model 3 resisted four times its own mass, even with an all-glass roof: that's the same weight as two adult African elephants. You can feel the low center of gravity in the Model 3 design of Tesla. The overall design of Model 3 is rigid and strong. It is charged via Tesla's innovative Solar Charging System. The charging stations are also available all across the country. EXO CARS brought Tesla in Nepal to promote it's environment friendly nature which can protect the Nepalese natural environment in the long run.
