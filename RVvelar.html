Range Rover Velar

The Range Rover Velar by the British car manufacturer is awarded as the 2018 world car design of the year. The Range Rover Velar design is revolutionary. The float roof design is simply amazing. The main feature of Range Rover Velar is Sports Command Driving Position, Head-up Display and two 10 inches high definition Touch Screens. The 8 speed automatic transmission is perfectly tuned for the feeling of smoothness while shifting gears. The EXO CARS brought Range Rover in the Nepali market to provide the luxury SUV experience for it's Nepalese consumer.
