ABOUT

EXO CARS is the one and only car dealership company which sells and distribute the cars of top major car manufacturing company like Tesla, BMW, and Land Rover in Nepal. It is our privilege to be able to provide services to the car enthusiast Nepalese consumer. Nepal's premium car dealership company EXO CARS was established in 2010 as a motto to provide premium luxurious cars to the Nepalese market. EXO CARS also organize charitable events like Drive to Inspire which help to promote Education among all the children who are unfortunate to receive formal school education. EXO CARS branches are situated all across the major cities of Nepal. The Headquarter of EXO CARS is located in Biratnagar. We also have branches in Kathmandu, Butwal, Pokhara, Bharatpur, Birgunj and Dharan.
